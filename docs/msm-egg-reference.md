## Scope

This reference covers the 30 Natural monsters used across the core Natural islands: **Plant**, **Cold**, **Air**, **Water**, and **Earth**.

It does not include Fire, Magical, Ethereal, Mythical, Seasonal, Rare, or Epic variants.

## Quick Timer Groups

- **Single-element Natural monsters:** from seconds up to a few hours, depending on the monster.
- **Fast two-element Naturals:** **Maw**, **Drumpler**, and **Fwog** are **30m** standard / **22m 30s** enhanced.
- **Most two-element Naturals:** **8h** standard / **6h** enhanced.
- **Three-element Naturals:** **12h** standard / **9h** enhanced.
- **Four-element quads:** **1d** standard / **18h** enhanced.

## Common Breeding Fails: 24-hour Natural Monsters

- **If the timer says `1d`, you bred a quad.**
- **If the enhanced timer says `18h`, same thing: quad fail.**
- Quad eggs in this reference:
	- **Entbrat** — Plant quad
	- **Deedge** — Cold quad
	- **Riff** — Air quad
	- **Shellbeat** — Water quad
	- **Quarrister** — Earth quad
